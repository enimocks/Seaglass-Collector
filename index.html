Seaglass Hunter!

  • There are 4 different seaglass images below. Each image is worth a random number of points. By clicking any one of the four pieces of seaglass, you add a specific number of points to your Total Score.
  • The point values of each image are randomized at the start of each new round, and are hidden from you until you click on them. It is up to you to figure out, and keep track of how many points each piece of seaglass is worth.
  • You are given a random target number at the beginning of each round. This is the exact number of points you should try to reach in each round.
  • You win the game by getting your Total Score to equal the exact value of the Target Number -- if you exceed the Target Number, you lose!
  • Good Luck!
