Seaglass Collector!

  • There are 4 different colors of seaglass below. Each color is worth a random amount of points.
  • The point values of each color seaglass are randomized each round, and are hidden from you until you click on each of them. It is up to you to figure out, and keep track of how many points each piece of seaglass is worth.
  • You are also given a random target number at the beginning of each round. This is the exact number of points you should try to reach in each round.
  • You win the game by getting your Total Score to equal the exact value of the Target Number -- if you exceed the Target Number, you lose!
